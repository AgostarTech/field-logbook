Learn More About This Project

This project is our Final Year Project for the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Mwenge Catholic University. It was proudly developed by Agola W. Tom, Robert E. Kipendaroho, and Millenium L. Ngao.

Our goal was to modernize field training supervision through a smart, secure and scalable logbook system. The system allows students to log daily reports, attach evidence, get feedback from supervisors, and have academic tracking in real time.
